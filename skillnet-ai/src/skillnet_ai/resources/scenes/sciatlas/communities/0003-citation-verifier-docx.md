## Common Task Patterns

- verify DOI/arXiv/PubMed/citation string
- normalize or hydrate a paper reference
- edit, convert, or assemble DOCX or PDF manuscript files
- generate publication-ready scientific figures for a paper/report
- prepare scholarly deliverable from citation to document artifact
